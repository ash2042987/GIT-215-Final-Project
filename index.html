Paris, France

Paris, a mustsee!

Experience

Experience the City of Lights! See beautiful monuments and the heart of France. Everything from florists everywhere to the smell of fresh bread baking. The ease of open air markets and convenient public transportation near you will have you want to experience your heart's desire. C'est la vie!!!

Fall in Love

Fall in love with the history EVERYWHERE. The architecture of each monument will have you at "awh." You'll get lost in the love in the air and endless walks to your next location in Paris. Paris is a place you'll never want to leave. “A walk about Paris will provide lessons in history, beauty, and in the point of life.“– Thomas Jefferson
